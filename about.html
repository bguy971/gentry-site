OUR PHILOSOPHY

More Than A Restaurant

The Gentry was imagined as something warmer than a typical restaurant — a place where wood-fired food, private domes, patio firelight and downtown energy create something people remember.

The goal was never simply serving dinner. It was creating a place people want to come back to.

THE CRAFT

Fire As
An Ingredient

Blistered crust. Bold ingredients. Wood-fired character. Everything starts with flame — not only as technique, but as part of the restaurant’s identity.

That warmth carries through the food and the atmosphere.

THE ATMOSPHERE

Designed For
Lingering

From dome evenings to patio nights around firelight, The Gentry was built for moments that stretch past dinner.

Slow evenings. Shared food. A place worth staying awhile.
